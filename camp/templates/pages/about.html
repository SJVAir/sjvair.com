SJVAir Collaborative

SJVAir is a network of high-performance (FEM) and low-cost (non-FEM) air quality monitors providing real-time data to disadvantaged communities across the San Joaquin Valley. SJVAir is operated by the Central California Asthma Collaborative, in coordination with universities and environmental justice organizations across the region.
