About SJVAir

Open is better than closed.

We subscribe to the ideals of open source software, hardware, and data, and believe these should be freely available to the public for the benefit of everyone. The code that powers the SJVAir website is open source and published on GitHub, where it can be freely used, adapted, and improved by the community. While not every part of the project is open source yet, our commitment is to make as much as possible transparent and accessible.
